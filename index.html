Engineering Physics - I

Physics is one of the most fundamental scientific disciplines, it is said to be the mother of engineering, as it generates fundamental knowledge needed for the future technological advances. Let it be product design or development nothing becomes successful unless and until we have an understanding of underlying physics. So, fasten your seat belts let's take you a level up with hands on towards ...Brains on !

Engineering Chemistry - I

Chemistry plays an important role in reinforcing the connection between science and engineering. Chemical engineering has been titled the "future of engineering".It is the product of an evolutionary process in which the majority of the other branches have played a role. It contributes in critical ways to the economic strength, security, and well-being of the nation. Let's start with our part of the contribution!

Engineering Mechanics

Engineering mechanics is the application of mechanics to solve problems involving common engineering elements, as well as the development of the ability to forecast the consequences of force and motion while performing engineering's creative design functions. The purpose of this Engineering Mechanics course is to expose students to mechanics problems as they are applied to realistic circumstances

Basic Electrical Engineering

Electronic engineering includes the practical application of the theory of electricity to construction of machinery, power supplies and systems which use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. VLabs will help you take care of your electrical needs by simply acting as a catalyst and helping you to adopt the right practices, so you are ready to beat the shock and check the volt.

About us

Virtual Labs will allow students to perform experiments using a virtual model of physical and chemical phenomenon to make such experimental activities more accessible and approachable, while providing freedom of exploration and an avenue to nurture students' curiosity.
